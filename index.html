The story

Like the moon, ZIRA reveals what hides in the dark.

"ZIRA" means moonlight in Amazigh — the language of peoples who read the sky long before screens existed. She grows from a specific root, North African, yet carries a message that belongs to no one: we all search for meaning under the same sky.

Where modern apps extract your attention, ZIRA gives first. Spiritual knowledge is offered freely. Trust comes before everything else. She is a friend for life, not a disposable product.
